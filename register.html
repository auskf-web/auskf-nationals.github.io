Register

Registration packets will be sent to each federation on January 31st. Please see this PDF for a more detailed registration timeline.

The most important dates for registration are:
  • 3/15/2017 - Federations submit the number of individuals and number of teams they wish to enter for each division.
  • 4/15/2017 - Tournament committee sends brackets to federations. Federations fill players into positions.
  • 4/30/2017 - Federations submit which players will be in specific positions in each division.
Pregistration forms - which should be completed by the appointed federation representative - can be found below. See the first page of each pre-registration form for the information one should have before beginning to fill out the form.
  • 2017 AUSKF National Championships
  • 2017 Junior Open National Championships
