Register

Registration packets will be sent to each federation on January 31st. Please see this PDF for a more detailed registration timeline.

For both the AUSKF National Championships and AUSKF Junior Open National Championships the registration process, important dates, and necessary forms are:

March 15, 2017 - Pre-registration closes. The registration will be compiled to determine the number of participants in each Tournament Division and the brackets will be constructed. See below for links to the pre-registration forms.

April 15, 2017 - The tournament committee will send each federation a confirmation list. The confirmation list will indicate Divisions registered and official participant numbers reserved for each tournament division (based on the March 15th reservation). Each federation will be responsible for assigning names to each participant number. A copy tournament division brackets will be posted to the Tournament website showing participant numbers and their respective bracket assignments without names. Refer to them to determine placement assignment for your registered members (an example will be posted to the tournament website).

April 30, 2017 - All Participant names shall be assigned to participant numbers and returned to the tournament committee. The tournament committee will perform a final eligibility review of participants.

May 15, 2017 - Bento order form (for all 4 days of competition) and gear (t-shirts and tenuguis) pre-order due. Sayonara Party RSVPs due. Please see the Forms section below for the bento, gear order, and Sayonara Party RSVP forms. See the Gear page for more information on items for sale.

Final brackets will be distributed at the Tournament Managers meeting.

Forms - which should be completed by the appointed federation representative by the specified dates can be found below. The introduction of each form contains the information one should have before beginning to fill out the form.

  • Due March 15th - 2017 Junior Open National Championships
  • Due March 15th - 2017 AUSKF National Championships
  • Due May 15th - Bento Order Form
  • Due May 15th - Gear Pre-Order Form
  • Due May 15th - Gear Pre-Order Form

Please email register@auskf-nationals.com with any questions or concerns.
