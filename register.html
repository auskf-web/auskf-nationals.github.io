Register

To register, a Federation Representative should email register@auskf-jrnationals.com. This Representative will receive access to a form unique to their federation where participants can be registered for the tournament and youth seminar. Volunteers, shinpan, bento, and tenugui will also all be noted and ordered on this form. We ask Representatives complete this form by May 13, 2016.

Please email register@auskf-jrnationals.com with any questions or concerns.
During the registration process, registrants will also be able to order a tenugui — design shown above — celebrating the 6th AUSKF Jr. National Championships. Please contact your federation representative for more information on ordering a tenugui.
